Our Classes

Mat Pilates

UP TO 10 PER CLASS

A full-body workout rooted in the classical Pilates method — no reformer required. Mat classes focus on building deep core strength, improving posture, and developing body awareness through intentional, controlled movement. Each class is suitable for all fitness levels, with modifications offered throughout.

Without Props With Props All levels welcome
Props may include resistance bands, Pilates rings, small balls, and light weights to add challenge and variety.
💪

Reformer Pilates

SMALL GROUP — MAX 5

Experience Pilates on the reformer — a spring-loaded apparatus that adds resistance and support to every movement. The small class size means you receive personalised cues and corrections throughout. Expect a full-body session that targets strength, flexibility, and coordination while being gentle on your joints.

Spring resistance Low impact All levels
New to reformer? Book an intro private session first so we can get you comfortable on the equipment before joining a group class.
🎯

Private Sessions

1-ON-1 · MAT OR REFORMER

Designed for those who want focused, individualised attention — whether you're recovering from an injury, managing a chronic condition, or simply want a program built around your body and goals. Led by Steph, a Registered Massage Therapist with a deep understanding of anatomy and movement dysfunction, private sessions integrate Pilates with a rehabilitation-informed approach.

Rehab-informed Injury recovery Postpartum Custom program
